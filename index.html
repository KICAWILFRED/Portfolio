About

My name is Kica Wilfred. I'm a Database Manager & freelance web developer in Uganda -- I specialize in web development, system analysis, database management, and cybersecurity. I enjoy crafting intuitive interfaces and efficient backends that solve real-world problems. I'm passionate about music, technology and basically everything that makes the internet tick.

As a student pursuing a Bachelor of Information Systems, I balance my studies with managing freelance clients and side projects. This dynamic mix fuels my passion for web development and keeps me inspired by the constant innovation in the IT world. My freelance web development roots are in Wordpress, of which I'm self taught. But I prefer to be challenged - that's why most times my websites are made from scratch with code, giving me full control over functionality and performance.

Whether you're a business owner looking to get started on an app or web development project with a freelance developer, a developer looking to collaborate on something cool, or just wanting to say hi, shoot me a message and let's work together.
